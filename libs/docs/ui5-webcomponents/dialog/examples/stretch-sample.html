This dialog uses the stretch property!

On mobile devices, this dialog will fill the entire screen, providing maximum space for content.

On desktop, it will stretch to approximately 90% of the viewport, giving users plenty of room to work with forms, tables, or other content that benefits from extra space.

Best practices:

  • Use for complex forms with many fields
  • Use for content that needs horizontal space (tables, charts)
  • Recommended for mobile usability when showing detailed information
