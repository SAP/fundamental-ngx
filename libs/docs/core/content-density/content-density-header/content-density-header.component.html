Content Density

Content density modes control the visual compactness of UI elements. Most components in the library support content density features.

The design system provides three content density modes:

  • Cozy - displays controls with dimensions large enough for fingertip interaction. Recommended for touch screen devices.
  • Compact - reduces the dimensions of the component, focusing more on the information displayed. Recommended for devices operated by a mouse or keyboard.
  • Condensed - reduces the dimensions of components and the space between elements. Recommended for small screens.
Some components do not support Condensed mode and will fall back to Compact. Use provideContentDensity() in your application's providers to enable global content density management with the GlobalContentDensityService.
