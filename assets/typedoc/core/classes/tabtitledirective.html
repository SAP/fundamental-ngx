Directive used to identify the template which will populate the tab header. Used to achieve complex headers that require more than a string.

<fd-tab>
     <ng-template fd-tab-title>
         <fd-icon [glyph]="'delete'"></fd-icon>
         <span>Tab Label</span>
     </ng-template>
</fd-tab>
