Directive to display a badge on a button.

Badges are small status indicators that can be added to buttons to show notifications, counts, or other supplementary information.

Usage:

<button fd-button>
  Button Text
  <fd-button-badge [content]="5" />
</button>
Note

Badge content should not exceed 4 characters for optimal display.

Note

Badges are only supported on emphasized, standard, ghost, and transparent button types.
