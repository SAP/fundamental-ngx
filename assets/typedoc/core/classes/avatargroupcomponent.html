type

type: AvatarGroupType = 'individual'

The AvatarGroup control has two group types:

group: The avatars are displayed as partially overlapped on top of each other and the entire group has one click/tap area. individual: The avatars are displayed side-by-side and each avatar has its own click/tap area.
