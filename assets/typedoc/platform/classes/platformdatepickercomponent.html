The Platform date picker component is a wrapper around fd-date-picker using platform form. It is able to provide form field functionalities as well along with fd-date-picker functions.

<fdp-form-group #ffg [formGroup]="datePickerForm"> <fdp-form-field #ffl1 id="birthday"> <fdp-date-picker name="birthday" type="single" [formControl]="ffl1.formControl">
