Selector: <fd-combobox-input>

The component that represents a combobox input. The combobox input is a composition of the "input-group" and "popover" components.

<fd-combobox-input [(ngModel)]="comboboxInputValOne"
                   [placeholder]="'Search here...'"
                   [dropdownValues]="dropdownValues"
                   (newItemClicked)="newItem()">
</fd-combobox-input>
