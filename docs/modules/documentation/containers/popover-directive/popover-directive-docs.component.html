Popover Directive

The popover directive is used by the fd-popover component. It constitutes both an abstraction of Popper.js and Fundamental-ngx features.

In cases where the popover component is insufficient, or even unneeded, this directive can serve as a powerful alternative.

<fd-popover>

Under the hood, the Popover component makes use of PopoverDirective. It abstracts some functionality in favor of consistency, but it may not be sufficient for every use case. If more flexibility is required, we recommend the use of the directive which can be found here.

Simple Popovers

Making use of simple popovers is very simple. Open an fd-popover component, place a control and body inside. You are no required to keep track of the popover state.
