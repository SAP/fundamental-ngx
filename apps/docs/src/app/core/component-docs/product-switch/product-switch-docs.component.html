Basic Product Switch inside Shellbar. Product switch with enabled Drag And Drop. The DnD functionality can be switched off by passing [dragAndDropEnabled]="false" to component. The event thrown on drop can be handled by binding to (productsChange). Product Switch With Less Than 7 Items When the product switch has less than 7 items, it has 3 columns and 2 rows. List Mode For narrow screens the Product Switch transforms to a List by itself. Add the [forceListMode]="true" to force the list layout, even on larger screens.
